Committees

UNHRC

The United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees is going to be the english high school comitte in POAMUN. The topic to be debated is: Addressing the Resettlement of Haitian Refugees in Light of the Growing Humanitarian Crisis. The chairs will be Gabriela Saute, this year's COO, and Isabela Marantes, a previous POAMUN General Secretary. This committee is enrolled in the UNHCR MUN Refugee Challenge, which is a competition organized by the UN where resolutions passed in MUN simulations are evaulated by UNHCR members and refugeess. For more information, see UNHCR MUN Refugee Challenge.

Security Council

O Security Council vai ser o comitê de portugues do POAMUN. O topico a ser debatido é: A Privatização da Força e o Uso de Mercenários. Os chairs deste comitê são Frederico Meister e Isabela Alano. Os dois são da universidade Unisinos, e tem experiencia liderando outros eventos de MUN.

ECOSOC

The United Nations Economic and Social Council will be the middle school committee of this year's POAMUN. The topic to be debated is yet to be decided. The chair of this committee is this year's General Secretary Marcela Pizzatto.
