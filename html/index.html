POAMUN
2022:
Age of Innovation

Description

POAMUN is an MUN conference that will be held at the Pan American School of Porto Alegre from November 25 to November 27. This year's theme is Age of Innovation, and the committees will be UNHRC, Security Council, and ECOSOC. The Security Council will be in Portuguese, and ECOSOC will be a middle school comittee. This is our third POAMUN, so we have experience in running smooth, high quality events. We hope to see you at this year's POAMUN.
